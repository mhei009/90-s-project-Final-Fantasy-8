Main Characters

Squall Leonheart


Squall is a cadet at Balamb Garden, an elite military academy in the game's world.
He is a member of SeeD, an elite group of mercenaries trained at the academy to undertake various missions and combat threats to the world.

Despite his young age, Squall is highly skilled and a natural leader, but he often keeps his emotions in check and maintains a stoic and distant demeanor.


Squall's gunblade, a weapon that combines a sword with a gun mechanism, is not only a tool of combat but also a symbol of his character.

His mastery of this unique weapon adds to his mystique and distinguishes him from other characters in the game.

Rinoa Heartilly


Rinoa is a passionate and idealistic young woman who is a member of the Forest Owls, a resistance faction in the small nation of Timber.

Her group opposes the rule of the Galbadian Empire and is dedicated to achieving Timber's independence.


During a mission to assassinate the Galbadian president, Rinoa crosses paths with Squall and his group of SeeD mercenaries.


Rinoa is known for her distinctive appearance, which includes her long brown hair, blue eyes, and a white evening gown-like outfit.

She is also notable for her pet dog, Angelo, who assists her in combat.

Quistis Trepe


Quistis is initially introduced as an instructor at Balamb Garden, an elite military academy where students are trained to become SeeD mercenaries.


She is one of the youngest instructors at the academy, and her character is known for her intelligence, discipline, and cool demeanor.

Quistis transitions from her role as an instructor to becoming a member of the protagonist group, joining Squall and the others on various missions and adventures.


Her combat skills revolve around the use of a whip as her weapon of choice.

Zell Dincht


Zell is a lively and enthusiastic young man with a passion for martial arts and combat. He is a student at Balamb Garden, an elite military academy where he undergoes training as a SeeD mercenary.


Zell is known for his distinctive appearance, with spiky hair, a tribal tattoo on his face, and a pair of gloves with fingerless sleeves.


Zell is a close friend and comrade of Squall Leonhart. He often provides comic relief through his impulsive and hyperactive personality.

Zell's combat style revolves around his martial arts skills, and he uses his fists in battle to deliver powerful punches and kicks.

Selphie Tilmitt


Selphie is a cheerful and energetic young woman who hails from the town of Trabia. Selphie is known for her positivity and her love of adventure and fun.


Selphie wields a nunchaku and has a unique Limit Break ability called "Slot," where she activates random abilities in battle, making her gameplay style unpredictable and exciting.


Her enthusiastic and carefree personality provides a contrast to some of the more reserved and serious characters in the game, like Squall.

Irvine Kinneas


Irvine is a skilled sharpshooter and a ladies' man with a cowboy-like appearance, complete with a hat and a long-range firearm.


He is a student at Balamb Garden, the elite military academy in the game, and is part of the SeeD mercenary organization.


Irvine's character is characterized by his laid-back and flirtatious demeanor, which often leads to humorous interactions with the other members of the group.


Despite his carefree attitude, Irvine is a capable marksman and is often relied upon for his sniping skills in various missions.

Secondary Characters

Seifer Almasy


Seifer is a fellow student at Balamb Garden, the elite military academy the other main characters are trained.


He is a highly skilled fighter and wields a gunblade, similar to Squall. However, Seifer's personality is characterized by his arrogance, hot-headedness, and a desire for power and recognition.


Seifer's rivalry with Squall is a central element of the game's narrative, and their clashes both in combat and in terms of their differing ideologies drive much of the conflict.

Laguna Loire


Laguna is a cheerful and somewhat clumsy man who is a former soldier from the Galbadian army.


He dreams of becoming a famous journalist and regularly writes articles about his adventures.


Laguna's storyline is presented in a series of flashback sequences that are experienced by the game's main characters through dream-like visions.


Laguna's adventures take him to various locations, where he gets involved in various escapades and missions, including battles against monsters and encounters with important figures in the game.

Kiros Seagill


Kiros is a loyal and silent companion who accompanies Laguna on his various adventures.

He is known for his distinctive appearance, which includes a mask covering the lower part of his face.


He doesn't speak during the flashback sequences, his actions and expressions communicate his character.

Ward Zabac


Is one of the companions of Laguna Loire and Kiros Seagill.


Ward is a large and imposing figure who communicates primarily through physical actions and gestures rather than speech. He is known for his size and strength and wields a harpoon in battle.


Like Kiros, Ward's character is distinctively silent during the flashback sequences, relying on expressions and actions to convey his emotions and thoughts.

Edea Kramer


Edea is as a seemingly benevolent figure, serving as the matron of an orphanage where many of the game's main characters, However, she becomes a central antagonist when she is possessed by the malevolent sorceress Ultimecia early in the game.


Under Ultimecia's influence, Edea takes on a cold and ruthless persona, becoming the acting ruler of the Galbadian Empire and causing widespread chaos and conflict.


She is known for her distinctive appearance, which includes a striking black dress and a large headdress.

Ultimecia


Ultimecia is a powerful and malevolent sorceress who serves as the central antagonist throughout the game.


Sorceress of the Future: Ultimecia hails from a distant future where she possesses immense magical powers. Her goal is to compress time and merge all eras into one, thereby achieving a state of ultimate power and control. To do this, she seeks to become the ruler of the present time.
