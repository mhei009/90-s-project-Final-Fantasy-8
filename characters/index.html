Main Characters

Squall Leonheart

Squall is introduced as a cadet at Balamb Garden, an elite military academy in the game's world. He is a member of SeeD, an elite group of mercenaries trained at the academy to undertake various missions and combat threats to the world. Despite his young age, Squall is highly skilled and a natural leader, but he often keeps his emotions in check and maintains a stoic and distant demeanor.

Squall's gunblade, a weapon that combines a sword with a gun mechanism, is not only a tool of combat but also a symbol of his character. His mastery of this unique weapon adds to his mystique and distinguishes him from other characters in the game.

Rinoa Heartilly

Rinoa is a passionate and idealistic young woman who is a member of the Forest Owls, a resistance faction in the small nation of Timber. Her group opposes the rule of the Galbadian Empire and is dedicated to achieving Timber's independence. During a mission to assassinate the Galbadian president, Rinoa crosses paths with Squall and his group of SeeD mercenaries. Rinoa is known for her distinctive appearance, which includes her long brown hair, blue eyes, and a white evening gown-like outfit. She is also notable for her pet dog, Angelo, who assists her in combat.
